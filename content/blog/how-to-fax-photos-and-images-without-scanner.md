## Why phone photos fail on legacy fax machines

Traditional fax devices expect **high-contrast printed pages**. Glossy screens, shadows, and JPEG noise turn into “dirty” gray blobs when sent raw. Professional workflows either use **scanner hardware** or **software preprocessing** before transmission.

## The professional workflow (software, not hardware)
